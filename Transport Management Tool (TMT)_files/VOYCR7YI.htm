“Transport Management Tool” (TMT) Gen3 - New Announcements


TMT Gen-3 Tool Go Live Updates (Bangalore4, Gurgaon):

We want to keep you all posted that TMT Employee App is now available for Android as well as IOS users using the app store (search "TMT Employee or Dell TMT"). Request all the transport users and SPOCs to download and start using it.
You can look forward to have more exciting features that TMT (Gen-3) has to offer like new user interface for better look & feel and easy to use.


Login Steps to TMT Employee App:
  • Enter your Dell Badge Id
  • Enter the Secret Key sent on your Dell Email Address (To generate the Secret Key, please click on “Generate Secret Key” button on the top of this page)
  • Click on the Login button
  • You will get an OTP on your registered primary mobile number updated in TMT
  • Once Authenticated, you will get a screen to setup your 4 digit app password
Note :
  • In case you forgot your app password, you can Logout and follow the same steps as mentioned above (Steps 1-5)
  • Android support version “Android 4.1” or above
  • IOS Requires 8.0 or later. Compatible with iPhone, iPad, and iPod touch.
  • App Features functionality & performance will be contingent on network and data speed
  • You can also download the TMT Mobile App User Manual on the top of this page

"Dell India Road Traffic Safety Management Policy"


Rev 01: June 2017

Dell International Services India Private Limited is committed to provide safe and secure transport services to its users.

We have adopted Road Traffic Safety Management framework to eliminate road traffic crashes and incidents within our transport boundary as well as inside Dell India campuses.

We are committed to continually improve road traffic safety management system and satisfy applicable requirements. Dell commits to performing to high standards when picking up and dropping employee, in a safe and secure manner.

Deepak Ohlyan
Vice President, Global Facilities Management, Dell
June, 15 2016
